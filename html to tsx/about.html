Our Story

Built by Pet People, for Pet People

We started Happy Trails because we couldn't find the kind of pet care we'd trust with our own animals. So we built it ourselves.

It Started With a Rescue Named Bear

In 2019, our founder Sarah Chen adopted a nervous rescue mutt named Bear. He needed daily exercise and socialization, but every dog walking service she tried felt impersonal. Walkers would change without notice. Updates were spotty. Bear came home stressed instead of relaxed.

Sarah started walking Bear herself, then her neighbor's dog, then three more dogs on the block. Within six months, she'd built a small community of pet owners who trusted her with their animals. That's when Happy Trails became a real business.

Today, we walk over 50 dogs a week across Denver. We've expanded to pet sitting, overnight stays, and cat care. But the philosophy hasn't changed: every pet gets the same attention and care that Bear got on that first walk.

What We Stand For

Our Values

You're handing us the keys to your home and the care of your family member. We earn that trust through transparency, consistency, and accountability every single day.

Whether it's a 10-pound chihuahua or a 100-pound Great Dane, every animal gets our full attention. We learn their routines, their quirks, and their favorite spots.
