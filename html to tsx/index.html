Why Happy Trails

Pet Care You Can Count On

We built this business because we saw too many dog walkers treat it like a side gig. This is our full-time commitment to your pet's wellbeing.

Insured & Bonded

Every walker is fully insured, bonded, and background-checked. Your pet's safety is never a question mark.

GPS-Tracked Walks

Get real-time updates and a map of every walk. You'll know exactly where your dog explored and for how long.

Photo Updates

We send photos and a summary after every visit. Because you shouldn't have to wonder how your pet's day went.

Secure Key Storage

Keys are stored in a locked safe when not in use. We take access to your home as seriously as you do.

Pet First Aid Certified

All team members are trained in pet first aid and CPR. If something unexpected happens, we're ready.

Consistent Walkers

Your dog gets the same walker every time. Familiarity builds trust, and trust makes for happier walks.
